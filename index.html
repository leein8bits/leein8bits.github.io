All About Me, Myself and I

         

I am Lee Gannon, a self taught web developer living in Georgia between Atlanta and Athens. I am always tinkering and building things, my mind is constantly thinking about how I can make or improve some already existing idea.

Web development provides an outlet for my passion for creating and learning. I enjoy learning and building new things and the feeling of success when a plan comes together. Web development is a space where I can grow, learn and become the best maker/creater I can be. In my free time I like to be with my family, play games, design games using Unity, draw and spend more time than I should browsing Reddit.

I can usually be found with the usual suspects: HTML,CSS, Javascript and other hooligans like Unity, Photoshop, Indesign and Illustrator.
